## Overview

Pensée is a personal growth and reflection platform that guides users through structured exercises, daily questions, and journaling prompts to improve mindset and mental clarity.  
The goal is to encourage self-awareness, emotional intelligence, and consistent personal development.

## Key Features

- **Daily Reflection**: Users receive thought-provoking prompts to reflect on their mindset and actions.  
- **Journaling**: A clean interface for capturing ideas, emotions, and goals.  
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual analytics to help users track growth over time.  
- **Privacy First**: All user notes are encrypted and accessible only by them.  

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js**: For building a fast and responsive user interface.  
- **TypeScript**: To ensure code reliability and scalability.  
- **Tailwind CSS**: For elegant and consistent styling.  
- **Prisma + PostgreSQL**: To store user reflections securely and manage growth data.  

## Challenges and Learnings

One challenge was making the platform engaging enough for users to keep a daily habit. Gamification and gentle reminders proved effective.  
Another lesson was designing the interface to feel **calm and inspiring**, rather than overwhelming.

## Outcome

Pensée is now being used by early testers to journal daily and track their progress. Feedback highlights its simplicity, emotional impact, and role in fostering self-discipline.
