## Overview

**La PEPINIERE SKM** is an e-commerce platform created with **WordPress** for an agricultural business.  
It enables the company to showcase its products, sell online, and manage customer relationships with ease.

## Key Features

- **Product Catalog**: Organized with clear categories, product images, and detailed descriptions.  
- **Shopping Cart & Checkout**: Integrated WooCommerce for secure transactions and smooth checkout flow.  
- **Stock Management**: Automatic inventory updates and low-stock notifications.  
- **Order Management**: Simplified dashboard to track and process customer orders.  
- **Customer Engagement**: Blog and newsletter integration to promote products and share agricultural insights.  
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for both desktop and mobile customers.  

## Technologies Used

- **WordPress + WooCommerce**: Core CMS and e-commerce functionalities.  
- **Elementor / Custom Theme**: For a modern, responsive, and tailored design.  
- **Plugins**: Payment gateways, SEO optimization, and performance tools.  

## Challenges and Learnings

The main challenge was tailoring WordPress’s flexibility to the specific needs of an agricultural company.  
I learned how to **combine WooCommerce’s power with a lightweight, optimized design**, ensuring usability even for customers with limited digital experience.

## Outcome

The platform allowed the agricultural company to **expand its market reach**, reduce reliance on physical sales channels, and build a stronger **digital presence** in its industry.
