## Overview

**Gestura** is a **stock management system** built with **Laravel**, tailored for businesses needing efficient control over their inventory.  
The platform provides tools for **tracking stock, managing requisitions, monitoring entries/exits, and generating detailed reports**.

## Key Features

- **Inventory Management**: Real-time stock tracking across categories and products.  
- **Requisition System**: Allows teams to create, approve, and process stock requisitions.  
- **Entries & Exits**: Simplified workflows for registering incoming and outgoing products.  
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Generate detailed reports on stock levels, movements, and usage trends.  
- **User Management**: Role-based access for admins, managers, and employees.  
- **Scalability**: Designed to adapt to businesses ranging from SMEs to larger organizations.  

## Technologies Used

- **Laravel**: Backend framework for robust application logic.  
- **MySQL**: Relational database for managing stock, requisitions, and users.  
- **Blade Templates**: Dynamic front-end rendering.  
- **Bootstrap**: For responsive and user-friendly interface.  

## Challenges and Learnings

The main challenge was ensuring **accuracy in stock tracking** while keeping the system intuitive for non-technical users.  
I learned to balance **complex business logic** with a **simple, clean user experience**.

## Outcome

Gestura has helped businesses **reduce stock errors, streamline requisition workflows, and improve operational efficiency**.  
It stands as a **complete and scalable solution** for managing inventory in real-world environments.
